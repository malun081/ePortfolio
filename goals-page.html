Aspirations and Purpose

We all have dreams, but we also have goals in life. A dream is somethinhg you wish for, but is something you will never achieve that is why is a dream. A goal, however, is something that you want realized, something you strive for, something that if you put your mind to, will earn and will feel amazing when you finally reach it.

I was never one for making list, but I have found it to be quite useful and feels somewhat more rewarding. Below are two lists, One containing my Short-Term Goals, the Second has Long-Term Goals. But what do we mean by short or long term? In this case, I decided that every year I have to complete one of these short-term goals and the long-term goals will fall into place as I finish my short-term goals

Short-Term Goals

  • Finish School and Earn my Degree
  • Learn Java Programing
  • Run a Marathon
  • Practice a Musical Instrument
  • Learn to Snowboard
  • Achieve desire level of physical fitness

Long-Term Goals

  • Maintain desire level of physical fitness
