About me

My sibilings and I moved here to the United States in October, 2000 and I have lived here in Utah since then. While in High School I enrolled in a program called School of Technology, which had us enroll in special computer classes learning different aspects of it. Such as computer programing, drafting and multimedia. When I graduated High School I had been doing Concurrent Enrollment getting college credit, but deciding what I wanted to major in became the hardest thing I had to do. As a consequence I studied quite a few things, but I loved every single thing about them. At the end I decided to studied physics because I felt it would give me answers to questions that plague my mind at the time and while this was true, I also wanted to do something to change the world. I transfered to the University of Utah and almost graduated, but personal life interfered with my studies and I dropped out of the program in my last semester. A year later I enrolled again at SLCC and now I'm once again pursuing a degree in computer science.
